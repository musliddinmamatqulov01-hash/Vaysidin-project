Who We Are

WE ARE IRONPEAK

Founded in 2012 by former Olympic athlete Marcus Reid, IRONPEAK was born from a single conviction: elite performance gear shouldn't be a privilege — it should be accessible to anyone with the will to push harder.

What started as a small warehouse in Portland, Oregon has grown into one of North America's most trusted fitness brands, serving over 50,000 athletes across 40+ countries.

We don't just sell equipment. We build the tools that build champions.
